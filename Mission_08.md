## Step 1

We need to show our good intentions by displaying a gift on micro:bit. For that you'll need a new block - it's called ``||basic:show leds||``.

```block
basic.showLeds(`
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    `)
```

## Step 2

Try to click on the grid to create a gift symbol! Like this:

```block

    # . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .

    . . . . .
    `)
```
